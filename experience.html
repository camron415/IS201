Experience

What I Have Done

Posted by Camron Trost on December 12, 2023

I am an individual who has shared in many diverse and unique life experiences. Among these experiences, I have learned the most important attributes and skills that are needed in today's world.

My life's experience may be very different from those of my peers. I like to believe that the most important thing is that everyone has their own life experiences that set them apart from everyone else.

My life has been nothing short of mindblowing lifechanging experiences that have shaped me to become the person I am today. I am looking forward to gain more experiences with my future employers.

A Mission

When I turned 18 I decided to serve a mission for my church. I was assigned to serve in the Chile Viña del Mar mission for two years. There I learned many important life lessons such as how to bond with people of a completely different culture and background.

I also learned a very important skill which is to speak Spanish. I found it to be very challenging in the beggining. After several months of practice and making mistakes, I began to finally see some improvements.

The best experiences that one can gain are those experiences that leave their life changed.

I have learned so much from my mission. After two years, I have finally been able to feel comfortable speaking in Spanish and conversing with people of different cultures and backgrounds.

Boy Scounts of America

Ever since I turned 11, I joined the Boy Scouts of America. I attended scouts from the age of 11 all the way until I turned 17 and during those long six years, I was able to learn many life lessons about how to survive in the wild, how to support a family and their needs, how to cook, etc.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.

Some of my favorite experiences from scouts where when we would go on campouts and adventure in the great outdoors. There were so many animals and so many bugs. However, I found that they were some of the best experiences that I had as a kid.

After six years, I had learned how to work hard and endure to the end. The moment I had finished scouts, I felt like a man. I think that this program is very important for every young man looking for meaning and purpose in life.
